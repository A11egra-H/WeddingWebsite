Frequently Asked Questions

More details will be added as we finalize plans.

What should I wear?

Garden formal attire. The lawn can be soft, so block heels or flats are best.

Are kids invited?

While we love your little ones, the ceremony and reception will be adults-only.

Where should we stay?

We have room blocks at Dream Inn and Hotel Paradox. Santa Cruz is popular in September, so book early.

How do we get there?

Monarch Cove Inn is 45 minutes from SJC and 90 minutes from SFO. Rideshare is available, and parking is limited, so consider carpooling.
